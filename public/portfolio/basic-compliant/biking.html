Biking

Back

Equipment: Bike, Helmet, Something to keep your pant leg from getting stuck in your gears (unless you want to shove the fabric into your sock), Sunscreen, Water, Backpack, Energy dense non-perishable food

Weather: Go biking in whatever you're brave enough to face. Beginners should stay at home during the snow. Biking in rain and especially hail kinda sucks.

The experience
Biking is fun as long as you don't get hit by a car or nothing breaks while you're on the road. Be aware of trails that don't allow bikes. Look for bike paths and bike lanes when you're in the city.
